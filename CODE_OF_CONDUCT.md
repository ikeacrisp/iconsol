# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

We adopt the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short: be kind, be patient, assume good intent, and don't be a jerk.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behaviour to `conduct@iconsol.me`. All reports are reviewed and investigated promptly and confidentially.

Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter.
